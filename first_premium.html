About this event


VanHack is doing a Meetup in Recife this month. We are bringing an event for Tech Talents who want to relocate to Canada or Europe and don’t know how. At the Meetup, you’ll have the opportunity to be part of a live Recruiting Fair where you will see opportunities from companies in Canada and Europe looking for talents just like you. One thing people usually are confused about is about the immigration process. How does it work to get a work permit? Can my family move with me? How long does the process take? A specialist on our Meetup will answer these and other questions. Another doubt people have is about the English. How important are your English Skills for getting a job abroad? We’ll have our partner, LingoCentre, responsible for our English Verification, talking about it. Apply for VanHack Recife Meetup here VanHack Recife Meetup will happen on September 20th, starting at 6 PM. Since we have a limited seat number, we need to make a selection of people who apply. To know more about the people attending to match the vacancies better to the Recruiting Fair, you’ll need to create and fill out your VanHack Profile. You’ll receive further instructions on your email. So, Recife, let’s rock this Meetup?
